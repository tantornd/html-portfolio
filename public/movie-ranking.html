Best Movies Ever Created

The objectively correct list of best movies of all time because it's rated by me

The Last 10 Years

Actually just a tearjerker since the first 5 minutes of the movie. RADWIMPS, the band that made the music for this show, really encapsulated the feeling of the fleeting passage of time.

April Come She Will

This movie represents the nature of humans as we grow apart and take different paths in life. A university couple, who one day might have had dreams of spending their life together slowly lose feelings for each other, as the inevitable hands of time pry them apart.

Look Back

Created by the author of Chainsaw Man, Look Back is a short movie that focuses on two mismatched pair of friends who both have the same goal ini mind. To one day become a manga artist. The two grow apart as one of them went off to univeristy to pursue learning background art. This movie is very thought compelling and will leave you with a new outlook on life.
